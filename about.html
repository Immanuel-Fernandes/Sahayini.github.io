About Sahayini

Our journey, our values, and our commitment to change.

Our Mission

To empower marginalized communities by providing access to quality education, healthcare, and sustainable livelihood opportunities. We aim to create an inclusive society where every individual can live a life of dignity and self-respect.

Our Vision

Motivated by compassion and love, we vision a society that empowers children, youth and women to live as dignified active citizens, enjoying their rights and sustaining the ecology of the environment in solidarity, justice and peace.

Our Journey Through the Years

2009 - The Beginning

Sahayini was founded with a mission to create lasting change in local communities.

2010 - First Education Program

Launched our flagship program, providing free schooling for underprivileged children.

2011 - Community Health

Initiated our first community health camp, offering free medical check-ups.

2012 - Skill Development

Started vocational training workshops to empower youth with job skills.

2013 - Expanding Reach

Expanded operations to two new villages, increasing our community impact.

2014 - Water Conservation

Launched a project to promote rainwater harvesting and water management.

2015 - Women's Empowerment

Formed Self-Help Groups (SHGs) to foster financial independence for women.

2016 - Corporate Partnership

Secured our first major corporate partnership to scale our programs.

2017 - Digital Literacy

Introduced computer training and digital literacy for rural youth.

2018 - Regional Award

Recognized with the 'Best NGO in Social Development' award for our region.

2019 - Sustainable Agriculture

Trained farmers in organic farming and sustainable agricultural practices.

2020 - COVID-19 Response

Launched massive relief efforts, distributing food and hygiene kits.

2021 - Post-Pandemic Support

Focused on livelihood restoration and educational support for families.

2022 - Mental Health Awareness

Initiated campaigns and counseling sessions for community mental well-being.

2023 - Youth Leadership

Inaugurated a leadership program to mentor the next generation of change-makers.

2024 - Tech in Education

Integrated smart classrooms and e-learning tools into our education centers.

2025 - Future Forward

Strategizing for the next decade of impact with a focus on climate action and sustainability.

Our Core Values

Integrity

We uphold the highest standards of honesty and transparency in all our actions.

Compassion

We are driven by a deep sense of empathy and a commitment to serve.

Empowerment

We believe in empowering individuals to take control of their own destinies.

Collaboration

We work together with communities, partners, and donors to achieve common goals.
